CARS MADE IN GERMANY

Porsche		 The Porsche 911 GT3 RS is a high-performance sports car that pushes the boundaries of engineering and design. Powered by a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine, it delivers a thrilling 525 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque.

Mercedes		 a renowned German luxury automobile marque. Synonymous with elegance, innovation, and engineering excellence, Mercedes-Benz has been a leading force in the automotive industry for over a century. The brand is celebrated for its commitment to safety, comfort, and performance, consistently setting new standards in automotive technology.

Volkswagen		 a prominent German automaker, is renowned for its diverse range of vehicles, from compact cars to SUVs and electric vehicles. With a focus on quality, reliability, and affordability, Volkswagen has become one of the world's largest car manufacturers. The brand's iconic models, such as the Golf and Beetle, have left a lasting mark on automotive history.

Adidas		 a German athletic apparel and footwear corporation headquartered in Herzogenaurach, Bavaria, Germany. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe.

Hugo Boss		 a leading global fashion and lifestyle company in the premium segment, offering high-quality women's and men's apparel, shoes, and accessories.
